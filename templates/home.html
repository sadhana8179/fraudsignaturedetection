About Us

Fraud Signature Detection is a revolutionary platform that helps individuals and companies verify the authenticity of handwritten signatures. Using advanced AI algorithms and image processing, we ensure your documents remain secure and fraud-free. Signature forgery is a serious issue that affects businesses, banks, and individuals. Our platform provides an easy way to verify the authenticity of signatures by analyzing image similarity, helping to protect you from fraudulent documents. With a simple and user-friendly interface, anyone can upload signatures and quickly check for possible forgery. We aim to make signature verification accessible.

Forged signatures are signatures created to look like another's, but not actually created by the original person. This is often used for unauthorized access, like forging a check to steal money. Signature forgery is a common form of fraud that can lead to serious financial and legal consequences.
